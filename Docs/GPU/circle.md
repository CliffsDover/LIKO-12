Draws a circle on the screen.

### Syntax:
```Lua
circle(x,y,r,l,c)
```
### Parameters:

* **x**: top-left x position of circle
* **y**: top-left y position of circle
* **r**: radius of circle
* **l**: default: false - circle will be filled, if true - circle will have just lines
* **c**: color of circle (0-15)
